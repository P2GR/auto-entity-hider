# Auto Entity Hider

Auto Entity Hider applies your chosen Entity Hider options when you enter supported regions or minigames, then restores your previous settings when you leave or disable the plugin.

## Highlights
- Uses your plugin-configured hide options only in supported areas.
- Restores your original Entity Hider settings automatically.

## How it works
1. Configure the hide options in this plugin.
2. Enter a supported region or minigame; settings are applied.
3. Leave the area and your prior settings are restored.
